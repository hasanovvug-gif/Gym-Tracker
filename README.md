🏋️ Gym Tracker — What is this app?
Gym Tracker is a smart personal workout log powered by an AI Coach. Here are the key features:

📊 Dashboard
Displays your scheduled workout for today with a "Start" button.

Shows overall stats: total number of sessions and cumulative weight lifted.

AI Coach tip: Automatically prepares a custom tip before every workout by analyzing your history.

💪 Active Workout
Set-by-set guidance: Complete a set, tap "Done," and move to the next.

Rest timers: 60 seconds between sets and 90 seconds between exercises, featuring a "+30s" extension button.

Real-time adjustments: Change your working weight on the fly during the session.

Smart monitoring: If your weight drops by more than 20%, the AI automatically asks what happened.

Skip logic: You can skip an exercise, and the AI will follow up to understand why.

Global timer: Tracks your total workout duration in the header.

📋 Program Schedule
A full list of all training days for the week.

Auto-highlight: Automatically pinpoints today’s workout based on your schedule.

One-click start: Launch any workout, even if it’s outside the regular schedule.

📈 History & Analytics
Volume Progress Chart: An interactive graph showing your growth across all workouts.

Detailed logs: Access weight, sets, and time for every past session.

AI Weekly Report: Generates an analysis of your results with tailored recommendations.

🏆 Workout Summary
A sleek results screen shown upon completion.

AI Performance Analysis: Evaluates the specific session and compares it to your previous ones.

Exercise Tracking: See at a glance if your weight increased ↗, decreased ↘, or stayed the same → for each movement.

🤖 AI Assistant (Chat)
A floating chat button available at any moment.

The AI is fully aware of your current plan, active workout, and history.

Ask anything about training, recovery, or exercise technique.

⚙️ Settings
Multilingual Interface: Toggle between Ukrainian / Russian / English.

Profile management, notifications, and privacy settings.
